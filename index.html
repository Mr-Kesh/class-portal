About Me

Hello! I'm Jeshua Dimawea, a junior Computer Science major at CU Boulder. I was born in Los Angeles but have lived in many places, including Indonesia, Germany, Alaska, and Colorado. My hometown, however, is Jakarta Cipete, Indonesia, where most of my family resides.

A Favorite Place

I really enjoyed my visit to Cefalù, Sicily in Italy. With its warm weather, clear ocean, and beautiful architecture, it’s a fantastic place. From hiking to swimming, the town offers a lot of activities, and the people are welcoming and kind. It’s a spot I hope to return to one day.
